First Aid Education and Interactive Learning Platform

Many individuals lack basic first aid knowledge, leaving them unprepared to handle emergencies effectively. This lack of awareness often results in an inadequate response during accidents or health crises, which could otherwise be mitigated or managed with proper first aid techniques. Additionally, there is a widespread absence of first aid kits in homes, buildings, and vehicles, further exacerbating the risks associated with emergencies.

This project aims to create a comprehensive first aid education website designed to increase public awareness, improve emergency preparedness, and promote the importance of having first aid knowledge and resources available. The platform will offer accessible and engaging content, including interactive modules, quizzes, video tutorials, and downloadable resources to equip users with essential first aid skills. By leveraging digital learning, we aim to bridge the gap in first aid awareness and readiness within the community.

Introduction

What is First Aid?

  • First aid is the immediate care given to an injured or suddenly ill person.
  • DOES NOT replace proper medical treatment.
  • Consists of temporary assistance only until professional help arrives.

PURPOSE OF FIRST AID

  • Reduce pain
  • Save lives
  • Promote recovery
  • Prevent condition from getting worse

PROPERTIES OF FIRST AID

  • Protect yourself - always prioritize own safety before providing help.
  • Assess a situation - evaluate the scene.
  • Arrange for appropriate help - Call 999 for ambulance / emergency help.
  • Give early treatment - Treat those who have more serious injury first.
  • Reassure the victim at all times - talk to them, build trust with the victim, keep them maintain consciousness.
  • Prevent cross-infection - wear protective equipment when treating a victim.
  • Assess the casualties - identify injuries and nature of illness affecting a casualty.
